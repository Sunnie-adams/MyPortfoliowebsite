Project Description

Techtronix Innovations, a leading player in the microchip and robotics industry, faced significant challenges in optimizing its operations, including streamlining sales strategies, aligning production with market demand, and identifying new opportunities for market expansion. This project demonstrates my ability to manage the end-to-end data analysis lifecycle, from preparation and visualization to strategic recommendation delivery. It highlights my proficiency in Power BI and data analytics to solve complex business challenges and deliver measurable impact. The dashboard enabled comprehensive analysis of sales performance, customer insights, inventory management, profitability, and market expansion opportunities. By transforming raw data into actionable insights, the project provided strategic recommendations to drive growth and operational efficiency.
